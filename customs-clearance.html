Port Clearance

We provide complete port clearance services at Ghana’s major seaports and cargo terminals, ensuring fast and compliant cargo release procedures.

Documentation Handling

Efficient documentation handling is essential for smooth cargo movement and customs compliance in Ghana. Our team manages and verifies all shipping and clearance documents like the bill of lading (BL), Commercial invoice, Parking list, Customs declaration forms etc…

Our experienced freight documentation team ensures accuracy, compliance, and timely processing to minimize delays and additional costs.

Duty and Tax Processing

We assist importers and businesses in calculating, processing, and settling all applicable customs duties and taxes required for cargo clearance.

In Ghana, several levies and taxes are applied and all these are accurately estimated to give importers and clients an idea of what they should expect in customs assessment.

Vehicle imports: open the estimator to enter your VIN and WhatsApp number (email optional). We will send your duty estimate on WhatsApp.

Final Delivery to Your Location

After successful customs clearance, we arrange safe and reliable delivery of your cargo directly to your warehouse, office, shop, project site, or distribution center anywhere in Ghana and neighboring West African countries.

Delivery Services Include:

  • Container haulage
  • Cargo transportation
  • Door-to-door delivery
  • Heavy cargo and project cargo transportation
  • Transit delivery to Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger, and Côte d’Ivoire

Types of Cargo We Handle
